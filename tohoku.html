TOHOKU •

東北

The Tohoku Region (東北地方, Tōhoku Chihō, literally "North East Region") consists of six prefectures in the north of Japan's largest island, Honshu.

About

Like most of Japan, Tohoku is hilly or mountainous, with the Ōu Mountains running north-south. The inland location of many of the region's lowlands has led to a concentration of much of the population there.

Things to do

It is well known for its countryside, mountains, lakes, hot springs, high quality rice and rough winters. Hiking, mountain climbing and hot spring get aways are where it's at!
