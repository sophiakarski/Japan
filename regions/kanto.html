KANTO •

関東

The Kanto (関東, Kantō, literally "east of the border") is Japan's largest plain and very densely populated. The large metropolises of Tokyo and Yokohama are located in this region, consisting of seven prefectures.

About

As the heartland of feudal power during the Kamakura period and again in the Edo period, Kanto became the center of modern development.

Things to do

Here, you could go up the Tokyo Skytree and Tokyo Tower, visit the Meiji Jingu, Imperial Palace and Ueno park, as well as wake up early to take a look at the Tsukiji market.
